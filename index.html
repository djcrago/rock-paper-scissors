Rock, Paper, Scissors... from the console!

To play, follow the below instructions.

  1. Press command + option + j. This opens up the "console" where you will play the game.
  2. Press the button below. You will be prompted to answer the question, "Rock, Paper, or Scissors?" Each prompt is a new round with the computer. A full game is best of 5, where ties don't count (so you may type in more than five choices).
  3. That's it! The console will display final scores and whether you won or lost!
